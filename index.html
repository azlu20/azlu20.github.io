About Me

Albert is a passionate Computer Science and Math student at Northwestern University. While maintaining his schoolwork, Albert loves to pursue personal projects such as making Neural Networks, web apps, and games.


During his time at college, Albert was involved in the Neuraltechnology Club as well as clubs such as the IEEE. Since collaborating in a group environment brings him joy and productiveness, Albert strives to work with a variety of teams.


Currently, Albert has taken advanced computer science classes such as Data Structures and Algorithms and Object Orientated Programming, and is hoping to pursue even more higher level classes such as Parallel Computing and Artificial Intellgience.


After his first year in school, Albert had the opportunity to work at USLI as a Software Engineer Intern where he learned C# and Angular, and even got to work on a dedicated team producing commercialized code for the company.


Outside of work and school, he is a tennis player that also loves to workout. When he's not playing tennis or hiking, you can likely catch Albert enjoying games such as League of Legends and Super Smash Bros Melee with his friends.
