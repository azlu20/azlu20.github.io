Lunar Games Studio

We are designing a resource centric puzzle game where previously encountered enemies suddenly become tools to solve the puzzle.

I had this game concept in mind for a while, but it was only until I took CS 376, Game Dev, and CS 377, Game Dev Studio, that I wanted to pursue this idea seriously.

Our team of 8 people consisting of artists, designers, and coders have put in countless hours, stumbling our way into a playable product.

We started this project in Nov 2021, and though we made a lot of progress, there still is a mountain of work ahead of us.

Nonetheless, I still look forward to the creative outputs we are trying to get the players to express.
